---

## 2. PROBLEM STATEMENT

> There is no single, well-designed, modern tribute page for Ram Charan that covers his entire career journey — from debut to Oscar — in a visually cinematic way that matches his mass image.

---

## 3. GOAL

Build a **professional, fully responsive tribute page** that:
- Tells Ram Charan's story visually
- Showcases his filmography timeline
- Highlights his iconic dialogues
- Lists his awards and global legacy
- Demonstrates strong frontend development skills for portfolio
